Why HEART?

Four reasons people stay: a real network, real impact, a research identity of your own, and a profile that stands out anywhere in the world.

01 · Global Network

Access a Global Network of Experts

We maintain deep mastery of Robotics, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Agentic AI, and Human-Robot Interaction — and operate as a transdisciplinary bridge. You will collaborate directly with social and cognitive scientists, philosophers, and designers within our international research networks.

02 · Real Impact

Translate Curiosity into Real Impact

Our mission goes beyond academic curiosity or industrial perspectives. We guide you so your contributions — from top-tier conferences and journals to patents — prioritize human and planetary needs, delivering value that lasts long after the research concludes.

03 · Your Identity

Define Your Research Identity

You gain insights that go beyond technical expertise. Through workshops and collaborative sessions you will understand your own values and aspirations — and learn to empathize with the real needs and realities of users and other stakeholders.

04 · Leadership

Emerge as a High-Value Leader

By bridging professional orientation and real societal needs, our members lead and innovate beyond conventional boundaries — emerging with a holistic leadership profile that distinguishes them in a highly competitive global job market, in academia or industry.

03

Qualities We Value

A strong foundation in AI, robotics, or programming is essential — but technical expertise is merely the baseline. We place the highest value on creativity, leadership, and critical thinking: the faculties that let a researcher see beyond the code.

We seek individuals who lead with open-mindedness and intellectual humility — respect for diverse perspectives paired with a commitment to mutual growth, and the cultural intelligence to navigate international research environments and the unique nuances of Japanese social norms.

We empower our members to take ownership of their research. We aren't just looking for contributors; we seek leaders who take initiative — with a foundation of empathy, inclusion, and responsibility, and a collaborative spirit committed to technology that truly serves others.

"True innovation is found at the intersection of technical excellence, imagination, social necessity, and ethical alignment."

"The ability to adapt and learn from others is more valuable than any specific technical skill."

If this mission resonates with you, don't overthink the requirements — just reach out.

04

The Professional Mindset

We view the mind, body and heart as deeply interconnected. Sustainability in research means taking care of your health — through proper nutrition, physical activity, and social interaction. Our lab culture encourages speed in refining ideas, but also values the time needed to relax and sustain long-term productive research.

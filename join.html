Enrique Coronado — AIST HEART

Join the Evolution of Robotics

We are architecting the Human-AI Future. Whether you are a student, social researcher, or engineer, HEART offers a inclusive space to grow and contribute to the next generation of AI and robotics systems.

Why join the HEART Team?

01. Access a Global Network of Experts

Our work transcends the scope of traditional laboratories. While we maintain a deep mastery of Robotics, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Agentic AI, and Human-Robot Interaction, we operate as a transdisciplinary bridge. You will collaborate directly with social and cognitive scientists, philosophers, and designers within our international research networks.

02. Translate Curiosity into Real Impact

Our mission is to create value that transcends academic curiosity or industrial perspectives. In HEART, we will guide you to ensure that your contributions—from top-tier conferences and journals to patents—prioritize human and planetary needs, delivering tangible value that lasts long after the research concludes.

03. Define Your Research Identity

Students and early-career researchers in our team gain insights that go beyond technical expertise. Through our workshops and collaborative sessions, you will not only develop a deep understanding of your own values and aspirations, but also learn to empathize with the real needs and realities of users and other stakeholders.

04. Emerge as a High-Value Leader

By bridging the gap between professional orientation and real societal needs, our members are positioned to lead and innovate beyond conventional boundaries. Whether you choose a path in academia or large-scale international enterprises, you will emerge with a holistic leadership profile that distinguishes you in a highly competitive global job market.

The Anatomy of the Skill you will Gain

01 — The Body

Master the physical interaction between machines and the world. Gain hands-on experience controlling industrial, mobile, and humanoid platforms using ROS 2. Design complex simulations and immersive interfaces with Unity, Blender, and JavaScript.

02 — The Brain

Architect high-level cognition and autonomous reasoning. Develop and deploy Agentic AI models using Python and PyTorch. Leverage large-scale simulation environments like Isaac Sim while utilizing AIST’s GPUs and supercomputers for massive model training.

03 — The Heart

Lead with empathy and inclusivity. We provide end-to-end mentorship across the research lifecycle—from conceptualization to publication—utilizing Responsible AI frameworks to ensure your innovations deliver meaningful social impact.

Qualities We Value

While a strong foundation in either AI, robotics or programming is essential, we believe technical expertise is merely the baseline. We place the highest value on creativity, leadership, and critical thinking—the faculties that allow a researcher to see beyond the code.

"True innovation is found at the intersection of technical excellence, imagination, social necessity, and ethical alignment."

As a global collective, we seek individuals who lead with open-mindedness and intellectual humility—a profound respect for diverse perspectives paired with a commitment to mutual growth. Thriving within our team requires the cultural intelligence to navigate the synergy between international research work environments and the unique nuances of Japanese social norms.

"In a world of rapid technological change and economic uncertainty, the ability to adapt and learn from others is more valuable than any specific technical skill."

We empower our members to take ownership over their research. We aren't just looking for contributors; we seek leaders who take initiative to drive the team toward shared success.

Transformative innovation requires more than technical excellence—it demands a foundation of empathy, inclusion, and responsibility. We look for individuals with a collaborative spirit and a relentless commitment to creating technology that truly serves others.

If this mission resonates with you, don’t overthink the requirements and just reach out.

The Professional Mindset

We view the mind, body and heart as deeply interconnected. Sustainability in research means taking care of your health—through proper nutrition, physical activity, and social interaction. Our lab culture encourages speed in refining ideas, but also values the time needed to relax and sustain long-term productive research.

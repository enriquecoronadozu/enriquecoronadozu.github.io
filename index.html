Responsible Agentic & Physical AI with Humanity in the HEART

I am a Senior Researcher with the Human-Evolving AI & Robotics Team (HEART) at AIST’s Integrated Research Center for Wellbeing.

My work begins by empathizing with human and planetary needs. Then, I translate these insights into sustainable, high-impact solutions.

I believe the true value of intelligent systems lies not in their capacity to automate tasks, optimize processes, or learn from interactions, but in their ability to enhance human well-being and safeguard our collective environment.

Designing intelligent systems that are
Safe, Ethical, and Viable.

My research moves beyond simply developing intelligent systems capable of acting, learning, and collaborating. I focus on the socio-technical alignment of AI and robotics, ensuring that every agent is socially accepted, ethically grounded, and meets the three pillars of impactful design: Desirability, Feasibility, and Viability.

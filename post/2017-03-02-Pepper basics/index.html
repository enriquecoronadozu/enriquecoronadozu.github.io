Pepper basics

Sun, Feb 5, 2017 Pepper

In this document a set of notes and useful links about Pepper robot are presented.

Nao safety:

  • ** Moving Pepper ** link

Turning Pepper on

  • Make sure Pepper is on a flat surface, providing enough space to move around.
  • Press the Chest Button once.
  • The boot process is completed when Pepper says “OGNAK GNOUK”.

link

Turning Pepper off

  • Put a hand on its shoulder or its back, in order to keep it in position.
  • Press and hold the Chest Button for 3 seconds, until the robot says “GNUK GNUK”.
  • The shutdown process is completed when all the LEDs are off.

In emergency mode - Forced switch off is not recommended in normal use. - Make sure Pepper is in a safe position, or he may fall. - Press and hold the Chest Button for 8 seconds.
